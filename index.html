About Us

"D Big" Homes was established in 1980 by its founder Mr Big. He felt there was a need in the Roanoke housing market for a company comprised of hardworking people dedicated to building quality homes that featured elegant designs demanded by the consumers.

The open first community, The Collections at Big Valley Ranch, was an enormous success. It offered designs for the executive lifestyle that were attractive to the young professional. "D Big" Homes had made its mark on the Roanoke housing market, and has continued to do so with its communities through the years.

Today "D Big" Homes continues to thrive in the Roanoke market with innovative, award-winning architectural designs with over 400 semi-custom homes a year in 10 neighborhoods around the metroplex. Soon will later expand its door to other Texas cities!

Our in-house team of architects and home designers are dedicated to creating homes that are spacious, beautiful and innovative. Their designs are based on more than 50 years of experience, as well as feedback from homeowners near the community.
